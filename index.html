About Me

Passionate about advancing robotics and AI

Hi, I'm Ibrahim, a PhD student in Autonomous Robotics at the University of Glasgow. I'm passionate about creating robots that don't just move; they understand, collaborate, and make a real difference. Also, I am a licensed remote pilot by the Malaysian government, licensed by MCAA.

My journey has been rooted in embedded systems, autonomous systems, computer vision, and deep learning, where I've gained hands-on experience turning complex ideas into working systems. I'm particularly fascinated by collaborative robots, human-AI interaction, and UAVs, exploring how intelligent systems can work seamlessly with people to solve meaningful problems.

In the long run, I hope to build a robotics institution dedicated to developing technologies that advance sustainability goals and empower people through education and innovation. I am also a licensed remote pilot, certified by the Malaysian Civil Aviation Authority (MCAA), which strengthens my practical experience in unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) operations and field robotics applications.

Featured Projects

Innovative solutions in robotics and AI

Development and Simulation of a Hybrid LTA Drone for Autonomous Pipeline Monitoring

This project presents a hybrid drone system for autonomous oil and gas pipeline inspection, addressing the limitations of traditional and drone-based methods in endurance and human dependence. The proposed design integrates lighter-than-air (LTA) and heavier-than-air (HTA) components to enhance flight time and stability.

YOLOv8 Computer Vision Autonomous Systems UAV GNVR LTA
Precision: 94.2%
MSE: 0.0023 m²
MAE: 0.0044 m
Design Document

Comparative Analysis of YOLO Models on Welding Helmet Detection

Welding is an essential process in industries such as construction, oil and gas, and manufacturing, but it poses significant safety risks. This work presents an automated helmet detection system using computer vision techniques to enhance safety compliance.

YOLOv8 YOLOv9 YOLOv11 Safety Systems PPE Monitoring
Best Precision: 93.3%
F1-Score: 89.9%
mAP@50: 94%
Design Document

P-DETR: A Transformer-based Algorithm for Pipeline Structure Detection

Pipelines are critical infrastructure for transporting oil and gas but are highly susceptible to defects. This project introduces Pipe Detection Transformer (P-DETR), a novel transformer-based deep learning model designed for autonomous pipeline detection and potential integration with aerial robotic systems.

Transformers Deep Learning DETR Computer Vision FNT Module
Overall mAP: 55%
Small Pipes: +8.6 AP
Recall: 73.9%
Design Document

Development of Smart Bidirectional Digital Counter with Light Controller System

With the growing demand for intelligent automation, this project was developed to modernize traditional methods of occupancy monitoring and energy management. The system automatically counts people entering or leaving and restricts access when maximum capacity is reached, with automated lighting control to reduce energy consumption.

Arduino Atmega328P Embedded Systems IoT Automation Energy Management
Accuracy: High
Energy Savings: Significant
Design Document

Ultrasonic Radar for Obstacle Detection

This project was developed to explore the principles of object detection and distance measurement using ultrasonic sensing technology. The Ultrasonic Radar System detects obstacles and measures non-contact distances ranging from 2 cm to 40 cm, scanning across an angular range of 15° to 165°.

Arduino HC-SR04 Sensor Integration Processing IDE Servo Control Data Visualization
Range: 2-40 cm
Angular Range: 150°
Design Document
